You can do it by defining a Form in ufl
e.g.

v = TestFunction(FE)
u = Coefficient(FE)
N = TrialFunction(FE)
a = u**3*v*dx
F = N*v*dx

Then you assign your 'u' to 'a' and solve a == F for your function N.
