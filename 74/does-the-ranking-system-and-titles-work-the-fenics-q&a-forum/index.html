Best answer

Users get points by asking questions, answering and voting etc:

Based on the total number of points, a user qualifies to the following titles:

FEniCS Novice: 100 points
FEniCS User: 1000 points
FEniCS Expert: 10000 points
FEniCS Guru: 100000 points
FEniCS Grand Master: 1000000 points
